Services provided for COVID patients

  • CDC (Centers for Disease Control and Prevention)

    Provides comprehensive information on COVID-19, including guidance for individuals, healthcare professionals, and communities.

  • WHO (World Health Organization)

    Offers global updates, guidance, and resources for handling the COVID-19 pandemic.

  • COVID-19 Vaccine Finder

    Helps individuals find COVID-19 vaccination sites near them, including pharmacies, clinics, and health departments.

  • COVID Symptom Study

    Allows users to report their symptoms daily to contribute to COVID-19 research and provide insights into the spread of the virus.

  • Telehealth Services

    Many healthcare providers offer telehealth services for COVID-19 consultations and follow-ups. Check with your local healthcare provider or insurance company for available options.

  • Local Health Departments

    Local health departments often provide resources and services for COVID-19 testing, vaccination, and support services. Visit your local health department's website for more information.

  • Johns Hopkins Coronavirus Resource Center

    Provides global COVID-19 data, including cases, deaths, and vaccination statistics, as well as resources for understanding the pandemic.

  • COVID Act Now

    Offers COVID-19 risk assessment tools, data visualizations, and information on local conditions and interventions to mitigate the spread of the virus.

  • COVID-19 Global Tracker (Reuters)

    Provides interactive maps and data visualizations to track the global spread of COVID-19 cases, deaths, and vaccination rates.

  • Healthline COVID-19 Hub

    Offers articles, guides, and resources on COVID-19 symptoms, prevention, testing, treatment, and mental health support.
